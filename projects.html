exreport R package
Fast, Reliable and Elegant Reproducible Research

Visit project Website View it in GitHub

exreport started as a "bunch of useful scripts" that one colleage and me were developing to make our life much easier during our grad student period. It became something bigger, structurated and at the end a fully mature tool that has been very valuable to ourselves and our lab colleages. We hope this project can spread out and be of use to other scientists as well.

"exreport has been conceived to obtain robust and transparent reproducibility when developing scientific publishable results. Describe your experiments and your analysis in an exreport script and reproduce publish-ready descriptive statistics, tables, plots and many more with a single click."
