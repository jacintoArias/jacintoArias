About me

I'm a PhD student based in Albacete, Spain.

I got my BSc and MSc in Computer Science from the University of Castilla-La Mancha in 2012 and 2013 respectively.

I am a member of the Intelligent Systems and Data Mining group (SIMD) at the Albacete Research Institute for Informatics in the University of Castilla-La Mancha. I am currently funded by a Spanish MECD FPU grant.

My research is focused on Artificial Intelligence and Data Mining, concretely in the field of Bayesian Networks, scalable applications and Big Data.

In this page I keep an updated reference of my work including publications, related software, and utilities. Please refer to the Research page for a more detailed information of my current activity.

Also, I have an updated CV available here. Don't hesitate to contact me any time for any matter, I will be pleased to help.
