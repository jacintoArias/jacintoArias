Given that my life is not (almost) entirely dedicated to my professional activity, I'd like to use this page to share with the community some of my hobbies and interests.



Music

I usually spend most of my spare time trying to be an amateur musician, mainly as a guitarist. My influences are wide, from 70's classics Pink Floyd, Genesis or King Crimson to modern Prog Rock bands like Porcupine Tree. I'm also an enthusiast of Southern Rock and North America's Folk and Bluegrass music.

Please check out my actual band Recombine if you are interested, a post/prog-rock instrumental trio sharing many of my influences (not the country music I swear). You can listen to our albums on Spotify or Bandcamp and watch our covers and live shows on our YouTube channel. Hope you enjoy.

Mechanical Puzzles


I've always been captivated by puzzles and other logic problems. I'd like to share a few pictures of my rubik-based puzzle collection (I know it has a modest size compared to others you can find in the web). If you are interested in this kind of puzzle there is a lot of information and a vibrant online community.
